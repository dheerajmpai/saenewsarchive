PM to jointly inaugurate the second Integrated Check Post at Jogbani-Biratnagar

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, will jointly inaugurate the second Integrated Check Post (ICP) at Jogbani-Biratnagar tomorrow with Prime Minister of Nepal, Shri K.P. Oli.  The second Integrated Check Post  at Jogbani-Biratnag is  built with Indian assistance to facilitate trade and people’s movement across India-Nepal border. 

Both Prime Ministers will also witness the remarkable progress in Government of India assisted post-earthquake housing reconstruction projects in Nepal. Out of GoI’s commitment to build 50,000 houses in Gorkha and Nuwakot districts, 45,000 have already been completed.
