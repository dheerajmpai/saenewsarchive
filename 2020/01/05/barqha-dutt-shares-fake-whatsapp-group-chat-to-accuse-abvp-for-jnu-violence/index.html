Barkha Dutt shares fake Whatsapp group chat to accuse ABVP for JNU violence

A mysterious whatsapp group chat has been shared widely on social media to show that the ABVP is involved in the recent violence in JNU. The group chat contains no other message except a person saying “We have so much fun at JNU. Maza aagaya. Un salo ko Desh Drhiyo ko mar ke“. (We had so much fun hitting them the anti-nationalists)

A similar image was shared by Barkha Dutt but it was cropped to show only the message while hiding the rest of the chat history. The message she shared said “People in support are coming to main gate. Whaa kuch karna hai?”

A twitterati rebutted her claim saying the user is actually from Congress Party. Though South Asian Express could not verify the same from our on-ground correspondents at New Delhi

The chat was shared by JNUSU ex-president Geeta on twitter and was deleted short after. But the same was widely spread on WhatsApp groups which was brought to our notice by our correspondent from Delhi.

It turns out that the group name was recently changed to depict it as a pro-ABVP group. The group name was changed from “.” to “Sanghi goons Murdabadh” and then to “ABVP Chee Chee” and finally to “Left Terror Down Down”.

The South Asian Express could not verify the original intent of the Whatsapp group with the available information. But the recent changes in the group names clearly says the group was not solely run by ABVP members.
