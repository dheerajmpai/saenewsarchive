“Kashmir se Guwahati, Apna Desh Apna Maati!”- More than 2000 participated in a rally at IIT Bombay

Around 2000 students, professors and staff of IIT Bombay gathered on the eve of Republic Day to march with a 1000 feet long Tiranga. The rally covered most of IIT Bombay’s campus ended with the National Anthem at the convocation hall. This rally highlighted the Nationalistic soul of IIT Bombay and its role in Nation-Building through Science & Technology.

This comes days after IITB Director asked students to keep politics outside IIT campus and focus on Nation-Building via advancements in Science & Technology. The Researchers in the rally endorsed the view shared by the Director. Apart from “Bharat Mata ki Jai”, “Jai Bhavani, Jai Shivaji” and “Vande Mataram”, slogans like “Desh ki rakhsha kon karega? Hum krege hum krege!”, “Kashmir se Guwahati apna desh apna maati” and “Ek bharat shrestra bharat” were raised.

The slogans of ‘Kashmir se Guwahati apna desh apna maati’ comes after Sharjeel Imam’s controversial call to cut off Assam from rest of India
