PM greets former PM Dr. Manmohan Singh on his birthday

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted former Prime Minister Dr. Manmohan Singh on his birthday.

In a tweet, the Prime Minister said, “Birthday greetings to Dr. Manmohan Singh Ji. I pray to Almighty that he is blessed with a long and healthy life.”
