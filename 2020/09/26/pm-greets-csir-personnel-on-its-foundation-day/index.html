PM greets CSIR personnel on its Foundation Day

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted personnel of the Council for Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR) on its Foundation Day.

In a tweet, the Prime Minister said, “Greetings to all those associated with CSIR on its Foundation Day. CSIR is at the forefront of furthering scientific research and innovation in India. They have also been playing a valuable role in fighting COVID-19. Best wishes to CSIR for its future endeavours.”
