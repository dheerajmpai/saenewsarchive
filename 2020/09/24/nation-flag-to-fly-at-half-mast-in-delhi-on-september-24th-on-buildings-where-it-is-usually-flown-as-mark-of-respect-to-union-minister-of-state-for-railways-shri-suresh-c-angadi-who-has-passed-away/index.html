Nation Flag to Fly at Half Mast in Delhi on September 24th on Buildings where it is usually Flown as Mark of Respect to Union Minister of State for Railways, Shri Suresh C. Angadi who has passed away

The Government of India announces with profound sorrow the death of Shri Suresh C. Angadi, Union Minister of State for Railways, Government of India on September 23, 2020, at the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi.

As a mark of respect to the departed dignitary, the National Flag will fly at  half mast in Delhi on September 24, 2020 on the buildings where it is usually flown and on the day of the funeral, at the place where the funeral takes place.

The date, time and venue of the funeral will be intimated later.
