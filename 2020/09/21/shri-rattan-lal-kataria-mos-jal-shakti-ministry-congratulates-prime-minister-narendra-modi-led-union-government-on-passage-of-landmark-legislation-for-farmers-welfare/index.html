Shri Rattan Lal Kataria, MoS, Jal Shakti Ministry congratulates Prime Minister Narendra Modi led Union Government on passage of Landmark legislation for farmers’ welfare

The Minister of State for Jal Shakti and Social Justice & Empowerment, Shri Rattan Lal Kataria today congratulated Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi led Union Government on passing of the landmark legislation for the betterment and welfare of farmers across the nation by the Parliament. Shri Kataria said that the Bill empowers farmers to sell their produce to any trader or institution anywhere in the country at a reasonable price without paying any middleman or additional fees or charges to mandis which will increase farmers’ income. Shri Kataria further said that these legislations will also benefit entrepreneurs, traders and food processing industry as they will be facilitated,they can buy produce from farmers directly according to their own needs.

The Minister of State also said that there is no change in the current policy of Minimum Support Price (MSP) and even after passing of this bills, purchase on MSP will continue. Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has also made it clear that the MSP will continue. Shri Kataria asserted that determination of MSP is the priority of Narendra Modi led government which has increased the procurement of MSP by 1.2 to 2 times in paddy and pulses. He further added that these agricultural laws will provide various types of legal protection to the farmers and no person will be able to exploit farmers and their land.

Shri Kataria said that due to lack of mandis and not getting fair price of the crop, the young farmers who migrate to cities for their livelihood, will benefit by implementation of these agricultural provisions. Also, upon getting the suitable price of their produce timely, the youth will be further attracted towards participation in agriculture sector, it will also create employment and lead to inclusive development in rural areas.
