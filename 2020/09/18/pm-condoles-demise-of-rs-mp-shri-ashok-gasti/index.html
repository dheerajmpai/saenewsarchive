PM condoles demise of RS MP Shri Ashok Gasti

The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the demise of Rajya Sabha MP Shri Ashok Gasti. 

 “Rajya Sabha MP Shri Ashok Gasti was a dedicated Karyakarta who worked hard to strengthen the Party in Karnataka. He was passionate about empowering the poor and marginalised sections of society. Anguished by his passing away. Condolences to his family and friends. Om Shanti.”, the Prime Minister said.
