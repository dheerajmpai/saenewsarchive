President of India to Address Fourth Edition of ‘The Huddle’ – Annual Thought Conclave of The Hindu in Bengaluru on February 22

The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Karnataka (Bengaluru) on February 21 and 22, 2020 where he will address the fourth edition of ‘The Huddle’ – annual thought conclave of the Hindu On February 22, 2020.
