PM condoles loss of lives due to bus accident in Tamil Nadu

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the loss of lives in the bus accident in Tamil Nadu’s Tiruppur district

“Extremely anguished by the bus accident in Tamil Nadu’s Tiruppur district. In this hour of grief, my thoughts and prayers are with the bereaved families. I hope those who are injured recover at the earliest”, the Prime Minister said.
