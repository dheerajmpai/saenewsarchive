Prime Minister expresses pleasure on US President’s visit to India

Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi expressed his pleasure on the upcoming US President’s visit to India on 24th and 25th February 2020. He added that this visit would be a very special one and will go a long way in strengthening the friendly relations of both the countries.

 

In a series of tweets, PM said:

Extremely delighted that @POTUS @realDonaldTrump and @FLOTUS will visit India on 24th and 25th February. India will accord a memorable welcome to our esteemed guests.This visit is a very special one and it will go a long way in further cementing India-USA friendship.

India and USA share a common commitment to democracy and pluralism. Our nations are cooperating extensively on a wide range of issues. Robust friendship between our nations augurs well not only for our citizens but also for the entire world.
