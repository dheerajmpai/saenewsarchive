PM reviews situation prevailing in various parts of Delhi

Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has reviewed  the situation prevailing in various parts of Delhi.

“Had an extensive review on the situation prevailing in various parts of Delhi. Police and other agencies are working on the ground to ensure peace and normalcy.

Peace and harmony are central to our ethos. I appeal to my sisters and brothers of Delhi to maintain peace and brotherhood at all times. It is important that there is calm and normalcy is restored at the earliest”, the Prime Minister said.
