Maintenance and Welfare of Parents and Senior Citizens Act, 2007

Thus, the Tribunals settled more than 80% of the cases in favour of the petitioners and more than 80% of the petitioners have shown their satisfaction to the orders of the Maintenance Tribunals.

 

This information was given by Minister of State for Social Justice and Empowerment Shri Rattan Lal Kataria in a written reply in Rajya Sabha today.
