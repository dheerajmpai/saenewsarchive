E-office Programme

 Indian Railways have signed Memorandum of Understanding with RailTel on 10.01.2020 for implementation of Phase-II of e-office programme in 34 Divisions, covering approximately 39000 users by end of June 2020.

E-office has been implemented in all the 59 targeted Railway Units in Phase-I, covering more than 53000 users. Details are as follows.

 

Rs. 86.25 crores have been allotted for e-office Phase-I project during last 2 years as per details given below:-

FY 2018-19 : Rs. 14.03 crores.

FY 2019-20 : Rs. 72.22 crores.

For promoting paper-less office work, Ministry of Railways has issued instructions to implement E-Office over Indian Railways. The use of E-Office by more than 53000 users, has resulted in reduction of consumption of paper, improved efficiency, ease of working and enhanced transparency in the system.

This information was given by the Minister of Railways and Commerce & Industry, Shri Piyush Goyal in a written reply to a question in Lok Sabha today.
