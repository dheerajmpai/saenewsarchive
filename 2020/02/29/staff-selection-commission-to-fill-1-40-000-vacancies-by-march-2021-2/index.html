Staff Selection Commission to fill 1, 40, 000 vacancies by March 2021

Chairman of the Staff Selection Commission Shri Braj Raj Sharma today met the Union Minister of State for Development of North Eastern Region (I/C), Prime Ministerâ€™s Office, Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions, Atomic Energy and Space Dr Jitendra Singh here and apprised him that the Commission will fill about one lakh forty thousand vacancies in Group-B &C for various Ministries/Departments and Offices of Government of India by March 2021. He said, both Gazetted and Non-Gazetted posts, besides Non-Technical ones will be filled up in a phased manner.

While, in the year 2019-20, as on 28-02-2020, the commission has already recommended 14,611 candidates for appointment in the Government of India, till Jun-2020, the Commission is likely to declare results for filling up of about additional 85,000 posts in various Ministries/ /Departments/Offices of the Government of India. Shri Sharma informed that additional 40,000 vacancies are likely to be filled up by the Commission in the remaining part of the Financial Year 2020-21 ( i.e. from Jul-20 to Mar-21).

The Staff selection Commission makes recruitment for:

(i) Group-B (Gazetted) posts in the Indian Audit and Accounts Department.

(i) Group-B (Non- Gazetted) Posts for various Ministries/Departments/Offices of Government of India.

(iii) Group-C (Non-Technical) posts for various Ministries/Departments/Offices of Government of India.
