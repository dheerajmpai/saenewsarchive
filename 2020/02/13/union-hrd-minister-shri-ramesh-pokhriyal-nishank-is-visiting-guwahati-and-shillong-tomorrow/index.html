Union HRD Minister Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ‘Nishank’ is visiting Guwahati and Shillong tomorrow

Union Minister of HRD Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal Nishank will visit North East to meet and interact with the students tomorrow. During this visit Union Minister will also review the progress of ongoing projects of MHRD in Guwahati & Shillong.

To encourage stress free education Shri Nishank will hold an interactive session Yuva Samvaad with students in Guwahati. During his visit to IIT Guwahati Union Minister will review the progress of ongoing projects and meet the heads of CBSE, KVS, NVS, NIOS and Central University, Assam. Beside this the Union Minister will also visit NIT, Meghalaya Campus and attend various E-Launching programmes at North-Eastern Hill University Campus in Shillong.

The government of India is committed to provide better infrastructure to educational institutions, In this direction the HRD Minister will inaugurate NRL- Centre of Excellence and estate building at IIT Guwahati. To promote the research work of students Union Minister will also see the innovative projects of students and will dedicate the innovations of IIT Guwahati to the Nation.
