Under the Khelo India Scheme, Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports has sanctioned 179 sports infrastructure projects including playfields in rural areas, for development of sports infrastructure facilities in the States / Union Territories (UTs) of the country. State/UT wise details are placed at Annexure – II. Further, this Ministry has introduced geo-tagging of all playfields in the country and sharing the information with the public, which is in progress. So far 3005 playfields have been geo-tagged across the country, which includes 94 in West Bengal.
