President of India Pays Homage to Dr Zakir Hussain on his Birth Anniversary

The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, paid homage to Dr Zakir Hussain, former President of India, on his birth anniversary at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (February 8, 2020). He paid floral tributes in front of a portrait of Dr Zakir Hussain at Rashtrapati Bhavan.
