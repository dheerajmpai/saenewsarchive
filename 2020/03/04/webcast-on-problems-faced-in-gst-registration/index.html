Webcast on problems faced in “GST Registration”

As part of a series of webcasts for addressing the queries of taxpayers on issues pertaining to Goods and Service Tax (GST) and Customs, Directorate General of Systems, CBIC and Directorate General of Taxpayer Services, CBIC are organizing a webcast on the problems faced in “GST Registration”. The said webcast will be held on 05.03.2020 (Thursday) from 11:00 A.M to 01:00 P.M. The YouTube URL for the webcast would be:
