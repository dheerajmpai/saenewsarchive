Elephant deaths by train accidentsshowing a declining trend

Due to concerted efforts taken by the Central and State Governments the elephant deaths by train accidents has been reducing in the recent years. This was stated by Shri Babul Supriyo,Minister of State in the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change,in a written reply in Lok Sabha today.
