Export of Masks, Ventilators and textile raw material for masks and coveralls prohibited

The export of Ventilators, Surgical/Disposable Masks  (2/3 ply) and Textile raw material used for masks and coveralls  has been prohibited with immediate effect.  A Notification (No.52/2015-2020) issued by Directorate General of Foreign Trade, Ministry of Commerce & Industry says that amendments in this regard have been made in the Export Policy.  All other items except surgical/disposable (2/3 ply) masks allowed in the Notification No.48 dated 25.2.2020 shall remain free for exports.
