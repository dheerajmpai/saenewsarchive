Assistance to Artisans for Construction of Worksheds

Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) through Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) extends financial assistance to Khadi Institutions for construction of  Worksheds to Khadi artisans under “Workshed Scheme for Khadi Artisans”. The Scheme introduced in 2008-09 envisages providing sufficient space and congenial environment to artisans for smooth and fatigue free working leading to enhanced production and increased earning. The scheme is demand driven. The pattern of assistance under the scheme is as follows:

 

Component

Area / unit

Amount of assistance

Construction of Individual Workshed

20 Sq. mtrs.

Rs. 60,000/- or 75% of the cost of workshed (90% for NER), whichever is less.

Construction of Group Workshed (Min. 5 & Max. 15 artisans)

10 Sq. mtrs. per artisan

Rs. 40,000/- per artisan or 75% of the cost of workshed (90% for NER), whichever is less.
