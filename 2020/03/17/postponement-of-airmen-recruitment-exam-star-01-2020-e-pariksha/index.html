Postponement of Airmen Recruitment Exam (Star 01/2020 e-Pariksha)

Scheduled Test for Airmen Recruitment (01/2020) automated e-Pariksha was planned at 86 cities from 19 Mar 20 to 23 Mar 20.

In view of the outbreak of Coronavirus, various government advisories and Section 144 at many places, STAR e-Pariksha planned

from 19 Mar 20 to 23 Mar 20 has been tentatively postponed to the last week of Apr 2020. For latest updates www.airmenselection.cdac.in can

be referred.
