Appeal to observe Janta curfew tomorrow from 7 AM to 9 PM

In an effort to prevent the spread of the virus, appeal of Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi is being recalled to observe Janta curfew tomorrow on 22 March from 7 AM to 9 PM. 

To salute the efforts of those providing selfless service to the nation during this trying times, appeal of PM is being recalled urging citizens to stand at the doors and in the balconies of their houses at 5 pm tomorrow and express gratitude by clapping hands or ringing bells for five minutes.
