Export of Tejas Fighter Jet

HAL is currently producing Light Combat Aircraft (LCA) – Tejas, to meet Indian Air Force requirements.  In order to export LCA to other friendly countries, the following activities have been carried out:-

(i)       The platform has been showcased by way of flying displays at various foreign exhibitions and also briefing about the platform has been given to various foreign delegations who have visited HAL facilities.

(ii)      The company has responded to Request for Information (RFI) from overseas.  Formal Request for Quotation (RFQ) has not been received in order to process the proposal.

         This information was given by Raksha Rajya Mantri Shri Shripad Naik in a written reply to Shri Manoj Tiwari and Shri Chirag Kumar Paswan in Lok Sabha today.
