Team Trifed Van Dhan India shows the way ‘Work From Home’

Continuing working from home, located in 27 States of the country, a group of over 500 officials from TRIFED, an organisation under the M/o Tribal Affairs, have unleashed the power of Webinars to keep in touch with each other and keep moving and working.Working on their computers from home, they have raised the bar very high, achieving unbelievable results.

Braving all odds, “The Tech for Tribals”, a project to train tribal entrepreneurs at 16 premier IITs &IIMs of the country, was launched on line on 19 March 2020. This scheme will link almost 3.5 lakh tribal entrepreneurs living in villages to the premier institutions of the world.

The Trifed VanDhan GIS based website, the future medium of communication for 50 lakh tribal gatherers was launched on line for testing with 27 State teams participating, on 27 March 2020. TRIFED is arranging atleast 2 team meetings every day and the Team is reaching out to partners to keep moving forward.

Tribes India Sale figures already registered a 30 % increase over the previous year on 29 February itself providing sustenance to 1.50 lakh tribal artisans. Lock down for them has not been as hard.

Trifed completed setting up 1205 VANDhan Centers across 22 states of the country, employing 3 lakh 70 thousand Tribals in ventures to promote entrepreneurship, on 29 March.. It’s double the target of 600 centers. It is now consolidating the early start and improving quality and outcomes of these tribal start ups.

 

Team Van Dhan India was contacted on 27 through a web platform to carry on gamely with their works. A proposal is being prepared for the Government of India to consider to provide grants to distressed Tribal gatherers who have to do distress sales in these times.

The Trifed Social Media team and the Tribes Com team are working round the lock for releasing socially useful messages to the country with telling effect. We have reached 2.5 million people in three days.

The time has been also used to refurbish and relaunch the Tribes India E Commerce portal with 10,000 Tribal products. A new look Web Site will be there soon. Team Trifed is grateful to MoTA & MSME for transferring Rs 105 crores and Rs 17 crores to take our work forward.Team TRIFED proposes to set up a portal to feed those Tribal Artisans who need it coming soon.
