Performance Evaluation of Trifed

The Ministry of Tribal Affairs reviews the performance of Tribal Cooperative Marketing Development Federation of India (TRIFED) through regular meetings, MoU evaluation, Monthly Progress Report etc.  The Ministry has not undertaken any study to evaluate the performance of Tribal Cooperative Marketing Development Federation of India (TRIFED), which is responsible of marketing tribal products through the network of its own outlets, using E-commerce and by organizing tribal festivals / artisan melas.
