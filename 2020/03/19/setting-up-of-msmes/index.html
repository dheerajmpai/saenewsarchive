Setting up of MSMEs

As on 13.03.2020, the number of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) registered on Udyog Aadhaar Portal since 01 April, 2017 are 61.06 lakh. The State/UT-wise details are given in the Annexure.

Ministry of MSME facilitates access to finance for Micro and Small Enterprises through its schemes/ programmes namely Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP), Credit Guarantee Scheme for Micro and Small Enterprises and Credit Linked Capital Subsidy Scheme.
