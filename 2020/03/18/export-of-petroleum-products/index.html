Domestic oil and gas producers paid a total amount of approximately ₹34,300 crore to the Government of India as Cess, Royalty and Profit petroleum during the year 2018-19. In addition, total amount of royalty paid to State Governments during 2018-19 from oil and gas production is approximately ₹13,400 crore.

This information was given by the Union Minister of Petroleum & Natural Gas Shri Dharmendra Pradhan in the Rajya Sabha today in a written reply.
