President of India addresses 8th Convocation of Guru Ghasidas University

The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind addressed the 8th convocation of Guru Ghasidas University in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh today (March 2, 2020).

Speaking on the occasion, the President said that the main objective of education is not only to get a degree but also to become a good person. Incorporation of moral values ​​into education is necessary because education without moral values ​​cannot be beneficial for the society. It is the duty of every university to inculcate values ​​like honesty, discipline, tolerance and respect for law among students. Only then students will become true citizens of a democratic country.
