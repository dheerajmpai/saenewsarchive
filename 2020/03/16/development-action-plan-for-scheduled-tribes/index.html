Development Action Plan for Scheduled Tribes

41 Central Ministries / Departments have to earmark funds for welfare and development of Scheduled Tribes under various schemes. These earmarked funds termed Scheduled Tribes Component are between 4.3% to 10% of schematic outlays except in the case of Ministry of Tribal Affairs, where its entire schematic outlays is for Scheduled Tribes.
