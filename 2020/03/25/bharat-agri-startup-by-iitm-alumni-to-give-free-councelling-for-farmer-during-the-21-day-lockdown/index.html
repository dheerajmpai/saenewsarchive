Bharat Agri, Startup by IITM Alumni to give free councelling for farmer during the 21 day lockdown

[Note : All content on Coronavirus is released under CC0 License. You are encouraged to reuse the content. Attribution is appreciated, though not required]

BharatAgri, a startup founded by 2 IIT Madras Alumni, Siddharth Dialani and Sai Gole is providing free councelling to farmers during the 21 day lockdown period.

Siddhart, the CEO of Bharath Agri had tweeted, “We are providing free counselling to all farmers; how to manage the lockdown and save their crops. We need farmers for food security of the nation. We are starting a helpline for free counselling to all farmers; how to manage this 21 day lockdown and save the crop.”

The helpline would be provided in Hindi, Marathi, Telugu, Kannada and English. The helpline will be open all seven days a week from 9am to 6pm.

Helpline number : +91-9075-9075-22.
