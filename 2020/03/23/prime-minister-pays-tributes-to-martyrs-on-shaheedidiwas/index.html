Prime Minister Pays Tributes to Martyrs on ShaheediDiwas

Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi paid tributes to Bhagat Singh, Sukhdev and Rajguru on the occasion of the ShaheediDiwas (Martyrs’ Day) today.

In a tweet Shri Modi said, “A grateful nation shall always remember the great sacrifice made by them. Jai Hind”
