PM thanks President of Sri Lanka

The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has thanked  President of Sri Lanka, Shri Gotabaya Rajapaksa  for his  contribution to the COVID-19 Emergency Fund.

“Sincerely thank  President of Sri Lanka, Shri Gotabaya Rajapaksa  for contributing $ 5 million to the SAARC COVID-19 Emergency Fund.  Our cooperation will go a long way in fighting this disease effectively”, the Prime Minister said.
