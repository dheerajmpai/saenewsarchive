Ministry of Shipping launches new website-(shipmin.gov.in)

Ministry of Shipping has revamped its website shipmin.gov.in and launched it on 30th April, 2020. The new website is based on open source technology and is deployed on NIC cloud Meghraj. The website is designed as per Guidelines for Indian Government Websites (GIGW) issued by Department of Administrative Reforms and Public Grievances, Government of India. The new website has a well designed and dynamic Homepage. Social Media Integration with better video uploading facility is a new feature in this website.
