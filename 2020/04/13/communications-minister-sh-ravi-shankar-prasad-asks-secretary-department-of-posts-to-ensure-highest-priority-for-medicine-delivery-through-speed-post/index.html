Communications Minister Sh. Ravi Shankar Prasad asks Secretary, Department of Posts to ensure highest priority for medicine delivery through speed post

Union Minister for Communications, Law & Justice, Electronics & Information Technology Sh. Ravi Shankar Prasad instructs Secretary, Department of Posts to ensure that delivery of medicines through Speed Post is given the highest priority during lockdown. Communications Minister shared in a tweet today that all employees of India Post should be sensitized so that no one should face any difficulty in receiving or sending medicines.
