PM salutes CRPF personnel, on its Valour Day today

The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has saluted CRPF personnel, on its Valour Day today.  

Prime Minister said, “The courage of CRPF is widely known. On CRPF Valour Day today, I salute this brave force and remember the bravery of our CRPF personnel in Gujarat’s Sardar Patel Post in 1965. The sacrifices of the brave martyrs will never be forgotten”.
