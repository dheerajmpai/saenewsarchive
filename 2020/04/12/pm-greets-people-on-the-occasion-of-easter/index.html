PM greets people on the occasion of Easter

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted people on the occasion of Easter.

Prime Minister said, “Best wishes to everyone on the special occasion of Easter. We remember the noble thoughts of Lord Christ, especially his unwavering commitment to empowering the poor and needy. May this Easter give us added strength to successfully overcome COVID-19 and create a healthier planet”
