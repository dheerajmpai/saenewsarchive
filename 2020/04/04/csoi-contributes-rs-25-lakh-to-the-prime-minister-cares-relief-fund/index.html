CSOI contributes Rs 25 lakh to the Prime Minister Cares Relief Fund

India is currently fighting a war against the corona virus. Our determined fight is led by the honourable Prime Minister  Sri Narendra Modi, and all of us stand strong and united behind him. The Civil Serivces Officers Institute (CSOI),  under the guidance of Shri Rajiv Gauba, Cabinet Secretary-cum- Chairman CSOI, has made a humble  contribution of Rs.25 lakh to the Prime Minister Cares  Relief Fund for providing assistance to those impacted by the pandemic.
