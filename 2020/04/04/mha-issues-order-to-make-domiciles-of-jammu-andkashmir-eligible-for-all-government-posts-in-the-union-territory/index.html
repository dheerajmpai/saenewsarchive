MHA issues order to make domiciles of Jammu andKashmir eligible for all Government posts in the Union Territory

UnionMinistry for Home Affairs (MHA) had issued anorder on 01.04.2020 for adaptation and modification of State Laws specific to the erstwhile State of Jammu and Kashmir, to further facilitate the application of Central Laws to the newly formed Union Territory of Jammu and Kashmir.

MHA has furtherissued an order to make domiciles of Jammu and Kashmir eligible for all Government posts in the Union Territory.
