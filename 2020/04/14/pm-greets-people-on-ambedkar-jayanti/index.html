PM greets People on Ambedkar Jayanti

 The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people on Ambedkar Jayanti.

“बाबासाहेब डॉ. भीमराव अम्बेडकर को उनकी जयंती पर सभी देशवासियों की ओर से विनम्र श्रद्धांजलि।”, the Prime Minister said.
