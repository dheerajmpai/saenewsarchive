RSS’s Kalyan unit with the help of Team of Doctors distribute around 800 PPE kits to Police officials, Doctors and Paramedical Staff

On the 14th of April, RSS’s unit in Kalyan in co-ordination with Abhudaya Pratisthan, handed over 200 personal protection gowns to the police authorities for surveillance in corona affected areas.

Similarly On 15th of April, with the initiative of RSS and a team of expert doctors, the police authorities at Ulhasnagar, Ambarnath, in Kalyan-Dombivali Municipal Corporation area were provided with the information needed to prevent the outbreak of the coronavirus, demonstrating the use of this protective PP gown to protect the on-duty police from the spread of the coronavirus.

In this work, RSS Volunteers of the team as well as 14 doctors who were familiar with RSS showed interest in participation.

In Kalyan-Dombivli and Ambarnath-Badlapur area, this team of 14 doctors went to 16 police stations in the area and presented information along with a demo about the use of PPE kit to the police officials.

Similarly, in dealing with this situation, the team of doctors also resolved the doubts in the minds of the police authorities.

In this activity RSS’s, Kalyan District SanghaChalak Dr. Vivek Modak also participated and had a conversation with the police officials in Tilaknagar and Vishnunagar police stations to improvise the situation further.

To date, around 800 PP kits have been distributed to doctors, police, and other paramedical staff through the RSS’s unit in Kalyan.

The PPE kit is definitely going to prevent corona’s spread to the government employees on duty.
