IIT Madras incubated drone startup Eplane company works with Chennai Police to fight #Covid-19.

The deadly COVID-19 outbreak has put the nation under lockdown. As of today, more than India has more than 4067 positive cases and 109 fatalities. While the lockdown has slowed down the spread the security personnel are facing tough time to ensure people stay inside their home during the lockdown.

IIT Madras incubated drone startup, Eplane Company, has collaborated with Chennai police to help the police in public surveillance during the 21 day lockdown.

The Eplane company was founded in 2016 by Professor Satyanarayanan Chakravarthy and Pranjal Mehta. The company was ideated out of the National Centre for Combustion Research & Development Lab (NCCRD), the largest combustion research and development lab in the world. The company currently works on drone deliveries and urban aerial mobility.
