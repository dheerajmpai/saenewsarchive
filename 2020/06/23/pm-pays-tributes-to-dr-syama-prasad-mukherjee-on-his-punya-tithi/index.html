PM pays tributes to Dr. Syama Prasad Mukherjee on his Punya Tithi

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has paid tributes to Dr. Syama Prasad Mukherjee on his Punya Tithi.  

“मां भारती के महान सपूत डॉ. श्यामा प्रसाद मुखर्जी को उनकी पुण्यतिथि पर शत-शत नमन।“, the Prime Minister said.
