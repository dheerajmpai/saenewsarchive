Updates on COVID-19

During the last 24 hours, a total of 9,120 COVID-19 patients have been cured. Thus, so far, a total of 2,13,830 patients have been cured of COVID-19. The recovery rate is 54.13% amongst COVID-19 patients.

Presently, there are 1,68,269 active cases and all are under active medical supervision.

The number of government labs has been increased to 715 and private labs has been increased to 259 (A total of 974). The break-up is given as:

Real-Time RT PCR based testing labs: 543 (Govt: 350 + Private: 193)

TrueNat based testing labs : 356 (Govt: 338 + Private: 18)

CBNAAT based testing labs : 75 (Govt: 27 + Private: 48)

In the last 24 hours, 1,89,869 samples were tested. The total number of samples tested thus far is 66,16,496.
