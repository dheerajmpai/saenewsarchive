PM greets people on the occasion of Raksha Bandhan

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people on the auspicious occasion of Raksha Bandhan. 

In a tweet, the Prime Minister said,

“रक्षा बंधन के पावन पर्व पर समस्त देशवासियों को बहुत-बहुत शुभकामनाएं।”
