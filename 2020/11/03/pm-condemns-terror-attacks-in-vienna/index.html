PM condemns terror attacks in Vienna

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condemned the dastardly terror attacks in Vienna.

The Prime Minister said, “Deeply shocked and saddened by the dastardly terror attacks in Vienna. India stands with Austria during this tragic time. My thoughts are with the victims and their families.”
