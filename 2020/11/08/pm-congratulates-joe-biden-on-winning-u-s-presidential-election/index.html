PM congratulates Joe Biden on winning U.S. presidential election

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated Joe Biden on winning the U.S. presidential election.

“Congratulations Joe Biden on your spectacular victory! As the VP, your contribution to strengthening Indo-US relations was critical and invaluable. I look forward to working closely together once again to take India-US relations to greater heights”, the Prime Minister said.
