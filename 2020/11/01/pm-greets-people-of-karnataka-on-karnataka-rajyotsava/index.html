PM greets people of Karnataka on Karnataka Rajyotsava

The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people of Karnataka on Karnataka Rajyotsava. 

Prime Minister said, “Karnataka Rajyotsava greetings to my sisters and brothers of Karnataka. Powered by the strength and skills of the people of the state, Karnataka is scaling new heights of progress. I pray for the happiness and good health of the people of Karnataka.”
