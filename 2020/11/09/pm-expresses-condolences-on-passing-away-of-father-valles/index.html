PM expresses condolences on passing away of Father Valles

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi expressed condolences on the passing away of Father Vallés.

“Father Vallés endeared himself to many, especially in Gujarat. He distinguished himself in diverse areas such as mathematics and Gujarati literature. He was also passionate about serving society. Saddened by his demise. May his soul rest in peace”, the Prime Minister said.
