PM’S greeting on Ayurveda Day

The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has greeted people on Ayurveda day today. He will be inaugurating two Ayurveda Institutes today.

In a tweet Shri Modi said

“Greetings on Ayurveda Day. On this special day, will be inaugurating two Ayurveda institutes, located in Rajasthan and Gujarat. The programme begins at 10:30 AM. Do watch!”
