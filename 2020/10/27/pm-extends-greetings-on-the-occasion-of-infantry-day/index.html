PM extends greetings on the occasion of Infantry Day

The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has extended greetings on the occasion of Infantry Day today.

Prime Minister said, “Greetings to all ranks of our courageous infantry on the special occasion of Infantry Day. India is proud of the role played by the infantry in protecting our nation. Their bravery continues to motivate millions.”
