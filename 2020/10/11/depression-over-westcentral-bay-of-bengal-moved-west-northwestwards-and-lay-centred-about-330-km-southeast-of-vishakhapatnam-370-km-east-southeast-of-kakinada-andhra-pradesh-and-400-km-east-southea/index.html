Depression over Westcentral Bay of Bengal moved west-northwestwards and lay centred about 330 km southeast of Vishakhapatnam, 370 km east-southeast of Kakinada (Andhra Pradesh) and 400 km east-southeast of Narsapur (Andhra Pradesh)

According to the Cyclone Warning Division of the India Meteorological Department (IMD):

              The Depressionover Westcentral Bay of Bengalmoved west-northwestwards with a speed of 07 kmph during past 06 hoursand lay centred at 1730 hrs IST of today, the 11thOctober, 2020 near latitude 15.5°N and longitude 85.4°E, about 330 km southeast of Vishakhapatnam, 370 km east-southeast of Kakinada (Andhra Pradesh) and 400 km east-southeast of Narsapur (Andhra Pradesh). It is very likely to intensify further into a Deep Depression during next 24 hours.

              It is very likely to move west-northwestwards and cross north Andhra Pradesh coast between Narsapur & Vishakhapatnam during 12th October 2020 night.
