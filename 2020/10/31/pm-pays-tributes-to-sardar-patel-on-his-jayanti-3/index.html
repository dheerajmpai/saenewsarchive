PM pays tributes to Sardar Patel on his Jayanti

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has paid tributes to Sardar Patel on his Jayanti.

“राष्ट्रीय एकता और अखंडता के अग्रदूत लौह पुरुष सरदार वल्लभभाई पटेल को उनकी जन्म-जयंती पर विनम्र श्रद्धांजलि।

Tributes to the great Sardar Patel on his Jayanti”, the Prime Minister said.
