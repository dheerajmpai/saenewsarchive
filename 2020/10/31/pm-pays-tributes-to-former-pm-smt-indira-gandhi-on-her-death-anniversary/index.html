PM pays tributes to former PM Smt. Indira Gandhi on her death anniversary

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has paid tributes to former Prime Minister, Smt. Indira Gandhi on her death anniversary.

“Tributes to our former PM Smt. Indira Gandhi Ji on her death anniversary”, the Prime Minister said.
