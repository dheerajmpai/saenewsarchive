Union Home Minister, Shri Amit Shah Expressed his Gratitude to Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi and Union Agriculture Minister, Shri Narendra Tomar for Dedicating Variety of 17 New Seeds of 8 Crops to Farmers

Union Home Minister, Shri Amit Shah expressed his gratitude to Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi and Union Agriculture and Farmers’ Welfare Minister, Shri Narendra Tomar for dedicating a variety of new seeds of various crops to farmers. In a series of tweets, Shri Amit Shah said, “Today is a historic day for the agricultural sector as Shri Narendra Modi and Shri Narendra Tomar dedicated a variety of 17 new seeds of 8 crops to farmers. These crops will provide nutrition to the people and the nation will move from ‘Green Revolution’ to ‘Evergreen Revolution’.” 

The Union Home Minister said, “The Modi government is committed to make the agriculture sector self-reliant as well as providing nutritional security to the country. The nutritional value of these new crops will be three times higher, which will make a normal plate of food nutritious by adding essential nutritional elements such as protein, calcium and iron.”

Shri Amit Shah added that “Proper nutrition is the right of every citizen and the Modi government is working towards it day & night. These visionary decisions of PM Shri Narendra Modi ji will not only provide proper nutrition to the poorest of the poor, but also increase the income of our farmers.
