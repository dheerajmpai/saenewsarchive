PM condoles the demise of Shri Ajit Jogi

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the demise of Former Chief Minister of Chhattisgarh, Shri Ajit Jogi. 

“Shri Ajit Jogi Ji was passionate about public service. This passion made him work hard as a bureaucrat and as a political leader. He strived to bring a positive change in the lives of the poor, especially tribal communities. Saddened by his demise. Condolences to his family. RIP”, the Prime Minister said.
