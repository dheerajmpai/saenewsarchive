PM to hold meeting with State Chief Ministers

Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi will be holding the 5th meeting with State Chief Ministers via video-conference, tomorrow, 11th May, 2020  at 3 PM.

In a tweet, the Prime Minister’s Office  said,  “PM @narendramodi to hold the 5th meeting via video-conference with state Chief Ministers tomorrow afternoon at 3 PM”.
