PM pays tributes to former PM Shri Rajiv Gandhi on his death anniversary

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has paid tributes to former Prime Minister, Shri Rajiv Gandhi on his death anniversary.

“On his death anniversary, tributes to former PM Shri Rajiv Gandhi”, the Prime Minister said.
