Loans to Scheduled Castes

Department of Social Justice and Empowerment provides concessional financial assistance in the form of loans through National Scheduled Castes Finance and Development Corporation (NSFDC) and Industrial Financial Corporation of India (IFCI) under Credit Based Schemes and Venture Capital Fund for Scheduled Castes (VCF-SC) respectively.
