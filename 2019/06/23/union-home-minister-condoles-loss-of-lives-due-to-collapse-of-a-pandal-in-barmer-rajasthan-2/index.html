Union Home Minister condoles loss of lives due to collapse of a pandal in Barmer, Rajasthan

The Union Home Minister Shri Amit Shah has condoled the loss of lives due to collapse of a pandal in Barmer, Rajasthan.

In a tweet, Shri Amit Shah said:

“Pained to know about the loss of lives due to collapse of a Pandaal in Barmer, Rajasthan. I express my deepest condolences to the bereaved families. May the injured recover quickly.”
