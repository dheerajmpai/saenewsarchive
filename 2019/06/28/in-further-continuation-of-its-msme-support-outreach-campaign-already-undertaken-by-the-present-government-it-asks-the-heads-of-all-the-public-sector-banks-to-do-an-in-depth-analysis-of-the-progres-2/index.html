In further continuation of its MSME Support & Outreach Campaign already undertaken by the present Government, it asks the Heads of all the Public Sector Banks to do an in-depth analysis of the progress made and issues in availability of credit, still being faced by the MSMEs; To make all out sincere efforts to ensure that during the process of formalisation, the MSMEs may not suffer in want of credit which should be available to them as per the extant guidelines
