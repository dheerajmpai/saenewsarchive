The Fifteenth Finance Commission meets representatives of Karnataka PRIs

The 15th Finance Commission headed by Chairman, Shri N.K. Singh alongwith its Members and senior officials met today with the representatives of the Panchayati Raj Institutions (PRIs)  of Karnataka.

The Commission was informed that all 29 functions of PRIs as envisaged in the 11th schedule of the Constitution, have been devolved to PRIs in Karnataka and the State has been releasing funds currently as per the recommendations of the 4th State Finance Commission ( 2018-19 to 2022-23)

Karnataka has a total of 6228 PRIs, of which 30 are Zilla Parishads, 177 Taluk Panchayats and 6021 Gram Panchayats.

As per the recommendations of the 4th SFC,

The 14th Finance Commission had recommended for the period 2015-20, a devolution of Rs. 8359.79 crores to Karnataka as Basic Grant ( 4.64 per cent of total grants) and a devolution of Rs. 928.87 as Performance grant ( 4.64 per cent of total grants).
