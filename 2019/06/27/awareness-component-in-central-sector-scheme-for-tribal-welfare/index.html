Awareness Component in Central Sector Scheme for Tribal Welfare

Ministry of Tribal Affairs provides funds to the State Governments for implementation of schemes administered by this Ministry for the welfare and development of Scheduled Tribes. Total expenditure of Rs. 379.16 crore was made under the Central Sector Schemes of Ministry of Tribal Affairs (MoTA) during 2018-19.
