Own Buildings for Anganwadis

As per the information available, there are 13.73 lakh operational Anganwadi centres (AWCs) in the country. Telangana and Andhra Pradesh, 20255 and 25502 AWCs are in operation in the government buildings (including schools and panchayats) respectively and remaining AWCs are being operated through rented buildings and community premises.

 

Under the revised scope of Anganwadi Services implemented from 01.12.2017, the Central assistance for construction of new Anganwadi Centre buildings in rural areas is available in convergence with Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS). Central assistance for construction of 1 lakh new Anganwadi buildings in a year is Rs. 1 lakh per Anganwadi building for all States/UTs. This amount is reimbursable to States/UTs after completion of construction of Anganwadi building.  State-wise details are given below:
