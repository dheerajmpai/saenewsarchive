27,893 Proposals Sanctioned Through psbloansin59minutes.com Portal as on March 31, 2019

As on 31.03.2019, more than 50, 706 proposals have got in- principle approval and 27, 893 proposals have been sanctioned.

The Solar Charkha Scheme for MSME Sector was launched in 2018 with an outlay of Rs.550 crore to set up 50 Solar Charkhas across the country. 19 proposals have been received under the Scheme so far.

This information was given by Shri Nitin Gadkari, Union Minister for Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises in written reply to a question in Lok Sabha today.
