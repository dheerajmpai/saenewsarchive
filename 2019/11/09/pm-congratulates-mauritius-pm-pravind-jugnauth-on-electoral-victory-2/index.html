PM congratulates Mauritius PM Pravind Jugnauth on electoral victory

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated Mauritius Prime Minister Pravind Jugnauth on his electoral victory.

 

“Congratulations Pravind Jugnauth on your electoral victory. We have worked closely together to strengthen fraternal relations and development partnership between India and Mauritius. I look forward to speaking with you soon and to continuing our engagement”, the Prime Minister said.
