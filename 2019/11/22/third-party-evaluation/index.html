Third Party Evaluation

NITI Aayog is getting the third party evaluation conducted of Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY).

Under PMMVY, funds amounting to ₹7022.36 Lakh and ₹14,101.75 Lakh were sanctioned/ released to the Government of Andhra Pradesh during the financial years 2017-18 and 2018-19 respectively. The total number of beneficiaries availed during the year 2017-18 and 2018-19 in the State of Andhra Pradesh are 1,53,099 and 5,00,361 respectively.

 

This information was given by the Minister of Women and Child Development, SmritiZubinIrani, in a written reply in the Lok Sabha today.
