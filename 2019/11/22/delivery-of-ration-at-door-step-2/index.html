Delivery of Ration at Door-Step

For establishment of the Central Consumer Protection Authority (CCPA), draft rules on CCPA under the Consumer Protection Act, 2019 have been prepared and uploaded on the Department’s website for stakeholders’ consultation.

 As per Section 89 read with section 92 of the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, a manufacturer or service provider who causes a false or misleading advertisement can be imprisoned by a competent court on a complaint filed by the CCPA or any officer authorized by it in this behalf.

 

This information was given in a written reply by the Minister of State for Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution, Shri. Danve Raosaheb Dadarao in Rajya Sabha today.
