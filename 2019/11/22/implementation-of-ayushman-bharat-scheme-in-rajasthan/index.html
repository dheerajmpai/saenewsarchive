Implementation of Ayushman Bharat Scheme in Rajasthan

As reported by the state of Rajasthan on Ayushman Bharat – Health & Wellness Centre (AB-HWC) Portal, 607 AB-HWCs are functional in the State as on 18.11.2019.

Ayushman Bharat – Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) is being implemented in Rajasthan with effect from 01.09. 2019.

As on 20.11.2019, there are 2049 hospitals (Public hospitals – 551; Private hospitals–1,498) empanelled under AB-PMJAY in Rajasthan. District wise details are given below:
