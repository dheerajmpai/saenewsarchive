Chief Minister of Manipur Biren Singh inaugurates the first Handloom and Handicrafts Mall at Wangkhei, Imphal

Chief Minister of Manipur Biren Singh inaugurated the first Handloom and Handicrafts Mall at Wangkhei, Imphal. The Handloom and Handicraft is run by the Manipur Handloom and Handicrafts Development corporation Limited.

He quoted “Glad to have attended the inauguration of the first Handloom and Handicrafts Mall at Wangkhei, Imphal, today, run by the Manipur HHDCLtd. Handloom and handicraft sector hugely contributes to the overall growth of businesses in the State and 50 per cent is owned by women.”
