Bilateral Meetings of the Prime Minister on the sidelines of ASEAN – India Summit, Bangkok

Prime Minister Narendra Modi today held separate meetings with Thailand Prime Minister Prayut Chan-o-Cha and Indonesian President Joko Widodo.

Prime Minister Narendra Modi said he discussed with the leaders on ways to expand the cooperation with the two countries

Had an excellent meeting with Prime Minister Prayut Chan-o-cha. We talked about ways to expand cooperation between India and Thailand. I also thanked him for the wonderful hospitality of the people as well as Government of Thailand. pic.twitter.com/79pMhf8MV1
