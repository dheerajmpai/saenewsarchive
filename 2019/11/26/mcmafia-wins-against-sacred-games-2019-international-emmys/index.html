‘McMafia’ wins against ‘Sacred Games’: 2019 International Emmys

‘Sacred Games’ starring Saif Ali Khan and Nawazuddin Siddiqui lost the award for Best Drama series to UK’s ’McMafia’ at the 2019 International Emmy Awards on Monday. 

‘McMafia’ follows the journey of Alex Goodman, an English raised son of Russian exiles who is drawn to the world of organised crime. 

‘Sacred Games’ is the story of the connection between a cop and a gangster as they try to save Mumbai from cataclysm.

Australia’s ’Safe Harbour’ won against the Indian Netflix film ‘Lust Stories’ which was nominated for two International Emmys, TV movie/mini-series.

Radhika Apte was nominated for Best Actress which she lost to Hungarian actress Marina Gera for Orok Tel. Nawazuddin Siddiqui stars both in the nominated ‘Sacred Games’ as well as the winning show ‘McMafia’. 

‘The Remix’, was India’s entry in the Non-Scripted Entertainment category was defeated by UK’s ‘The Real Full Monty: Ladies’ Night’. Brazil’s ‘Hack in the City’ won the category Short Form Series.

The event took place at the Hilton New York Hotel in Manhattan. The star studded event was hosted by ‘Crazy Rich Asians’ actor Ronny Chieng.
