Vice President pays tribute to the martyrs of 26/11 Mumbai terror attacks

The Vice President, Shri M. Venkaiah Naidu has paid tribute to the martyrs of 26/11 Mumbai terror attack. Remembering the martyrs of the terror attack he said –

“My tributes to all those who lost their lives in the dastardly Mumbai terror attack of 2008. We stand in solidarity with the bereaved families.

I salute the bravery and devotion of our security personnel who laid down their lives to protect the motherland. The nation shall forever remain grateful to their sacrifices.”
