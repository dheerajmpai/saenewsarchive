Odisha to host Men’s Hockey World Cup in 2023

Odisha will host the Men’s Hockey World Cup in 2023 and the matches will be played in Bhubaneswar and Rourkela. 

The announcement was made during a ceremony conducted by Hockey India and Department of Sports, Government of Odisha at the Kalinga stadium here on Wednesday.

The Executive Board of the International Hockey Federation (FIH) had previously announced on November 8 that India will be hosting the Men’s Hockey World Cup in 2023.

Chief Minister of Odisha Naveen Patnaik took to Twitter to make the announcement after Hockey India’s selection of the venue for the International Hockey event.
