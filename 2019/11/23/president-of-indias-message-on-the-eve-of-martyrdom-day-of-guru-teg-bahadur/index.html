President of India’s Message on the eve of ‘Martyrdom Day’ of Guru Teg Bahadur

he President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind in his message on the eve of ‘Martyrdom Day’ of Guru Teg Bahadur has said :- 

“On the occasion of ‘Martyrdom Day’ of Guru Teg Bahadur, I respectfully pay my homage to him. 

The ninth Sikh Guru, Guru Teg Bahadur dedicated his life to the betterment of humankind and to promote a sense of unity, service and fraternity in the society. He worked to alleviate the sufferings of the people and fought against oppression. For this reason, Guru Teg Bahadur is aptly called ‘Hind Di Chadar’. 

On this occasion, let us resolve to spread the values of love, harmony, compassion, benevolence, and work for the well-being of all”.
