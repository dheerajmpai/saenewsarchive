President of India to Attend 550th Birth Anniversary Celebrations of Sri Guru Nanak Dev Ji at Sultanpur Lodhi Tomorrow

The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Punjab tomorrow (November 12, 2019) to attend the 550th birth anniversary celebrations of Sri Guru Nanak Dev Ji organised by Shiromani Gurdwara Parbandhak Committee, Sri Amritsar Sahib at Sultanpur Lodhi.
