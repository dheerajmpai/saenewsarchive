PM condoles demise of TN Seshan

Prime Minister Narendra Modi has condoled the demise of T N Seshan.

Prime minister said, “Shri TN Seshan was an outstanding civil servant. He served India with utmost diligence and integrity. His efforts towards electoral reforms have made our democracy stronger and more participative. Pained by his demise. Om Shanti.”
