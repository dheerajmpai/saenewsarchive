President of India to visit Sikkim and Meghalaya from November 2 to 4

The President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, will visit Sikkim and Meghalaya from November 2 to 4, 2019.

On November 3, 2019, the President will grace the 5th convocation of Sikkim University in Gangtok, Sikkim.

On November 4, 2019, the President will grace the 26th convocation of the North-Eastern Hill University (NEHU) in Shillong, Meghalaya.
