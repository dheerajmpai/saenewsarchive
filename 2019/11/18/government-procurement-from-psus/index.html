Government procurement from PSUs

Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) are under the jurisdiction of respective Ministries/Departments and the Department of Public Enterprises has not procured any items through Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) and has not placed orders with Central Public Sector Enterprises during the last three years.

This information was given by the Minister of Heavy Industries & Public Enterprises, Prakash Javadekar, in a written reply in the Rajya Sabha today.
