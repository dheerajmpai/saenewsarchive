Uddhav Thackeray takes oath as Maharashtra CM

Shiv Sena leader Uddhav Thackeray was sworn-in as the 18th Chief Minister of Maharashtra by Governor Bhagat Singh Koshyari at a grand ceremony held at the Shivaji Park.

Thackeray will be taking over as Chief Minister more than a month after the Assembly election results were declared on October 24. On Tuesday, Thackeray had been unanimously elected as the leader of the tri-party alliance-Maha Vikas Aghadi, an alliance of Shiv Sena, Nationalist Congress Party (NCP) and Congress.

Uddhav is the first member of the Thackeray family to become the Chief Minister and the third Shiv Sena leader to hold the top post after Manohar Joshi and Narayan Rane.
