PM pays tributes to former PM Mrs. Indira Gandhi, on her death anniversary

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has paid tributes to former Prime Minister Mrs. Indira Gandhi, on her death anniversary.

“देश की पूर्व प्रधानमंत्री श्रीमती इंदिरा गांधी को उनकी पुण्यतिथि पर विनम्र श्रद्धांजलि।“, the Prime Minister said.
