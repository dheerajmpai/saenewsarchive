Delhi CM flags off 104 new CNG buses

Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal on Friday flagged off 104 new CNG buses from a bus depot at Dwarka Sector 22.

The buses feature CCTV cameras, panic buttons, and hydraulic lifts for the differently-abled amongst other unique features.

The initiative comes four days before travel would be made free of cost for women on Delhi Transport Corporation (DTC) and cluster buses in the national capital.
