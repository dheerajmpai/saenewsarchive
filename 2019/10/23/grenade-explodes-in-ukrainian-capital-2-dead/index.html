Grenade explodes in Ukrainian capital, 2 dead

Two men died and a woman sustained injuries after a grenade exploded in Kyiv in Tuesday night, according to the state media.

The explosion was a result of negligence by two unidentified men who were found playing and mishandling a hand grenade next to a cafe, witnesses quoted to Sputnik news agency. Police and ambulance have been deployed at the spot and the injured woman has been shifted to a hospital. An investigation is underway.
