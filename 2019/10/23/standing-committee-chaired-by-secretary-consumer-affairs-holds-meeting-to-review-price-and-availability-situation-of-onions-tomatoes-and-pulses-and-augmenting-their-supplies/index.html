Standing Committee chaired by Secretary Consumer Affairs holds meeting to review price and availability situation of Onions, Tomatoes and Pulses and augmenting their supplies

 

The Standing Committee held a meeting today under the Chairmanship of Secretary, Consumer Affairs, Shri Avinash K Srivastava to review the price and availability situation of Onions, Tomatoes and Pulses and augmenting their supplies. It was attended by representatives of Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers’ Welfare, MD NAFED, Mother Dairy, Kendriya Bhandar, Representative of Delhi Government and senior officials of the Department of Consumer Affairs.
