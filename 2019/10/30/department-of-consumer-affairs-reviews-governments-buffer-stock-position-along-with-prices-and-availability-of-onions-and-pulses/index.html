Department of Consumer Affairs reviews Government’s buffer stock position along with prices and availability of Onions and Pulses

The Secretary, Department of Consumer Affairs, Shri Avinash K Srivastava today chaired a meeting of the Standing Committee to review Government’s buffer stock position along with the prices and availability of Onions and Pulses across the country.
