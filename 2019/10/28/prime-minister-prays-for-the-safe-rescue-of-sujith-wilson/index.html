Prime Minister prays for the safe rescue of Sujith Wilson

Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi prayed for the safe rescue of Shri Sujith Wilson. He said that he had spoken to Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu Dr. Edappadi Palanisamy regarding the rescue efforts underway to save Sujith. He adds that every effort is being made to ensure that he is safe.

My prayers are with the young and brave Sujith Wilson. Spoke to CM @EPSTamilNadu regarding the rescue efforts underway to save Sujith. Every effort is being made to ensure that he is safe. @CMOTamilNadu
