Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas has not issued any advertisement for opening of dealership/distributorship of petroleum products

It has come to the notice of Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas that some unscrupulous persons, by using name and logo of the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural gas, have issued Approval Letter for opening Gas Agency dealership/ distributorship through LPG VITARAK KENDRA.

Through this Press Release, it is clarified that Union Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas, Government of India does not issue any advertisement for opening of dealership/distributorship of petroleum products. It is further informed that Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas does not have any role in appointment/ awarding of dealership/distributorship. The Oil Marketing Companies, based on feasibility study, identify the locations and issue advertisement of dealers, as per the respective selection guidelines.

Accordingly, general public is advised not to take notice of such advertisement/ approval letter.
