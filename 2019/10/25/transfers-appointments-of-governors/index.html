Transfers & Appointments of Governors

The President of India is pleased to make the following appointments/changes:-

1.      Shri Satya Pal Malik, Governor of Jammu & Kashmir is transferred and appointed as Governor of Goa;

2.      Shri P.S. Sreedharan Pillai as Governor of Mizoram;

3.      Shri Girish Chandra Murmu as Lieutenant Governor of Jammu-Kashmir;

4.      Shri Radha Krishna Mathur as Lieutenant Governor of Ladakh

The above appointments will take effect from the dates they assume charge of their respective offices.
