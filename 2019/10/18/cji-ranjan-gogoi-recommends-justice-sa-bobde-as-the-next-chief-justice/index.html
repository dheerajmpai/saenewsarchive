CJI Ranjan Gogoi recommends Justice SA Bobde as the next Chief Justice

The Chief Justice of India (CJI) Ranjan Gogoi, as per tradition has written a letter of appointment to the ministry of law and justice recommending the name of Justice Sharad Arvind Bobde as his immediate successor. CJI Gogoi will be retiring on November 17 after which Justice Bobde is expected to take oath as the 47th CJI on 18 November to serve the post for one year and five months till April 2021.
