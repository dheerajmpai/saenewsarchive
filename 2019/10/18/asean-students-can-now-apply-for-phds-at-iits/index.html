ASEAN students can now apply for PhDs at IITs

Indian Institute of Technology Delhi declared that all IITs will open admissions for students from ASEAN countries for their PhD programs. The application portal is set open for all branches in all IITs.

The total duration of the program would be 5 years. The monthly stipend is Rs 31,000 for the first two years and Rs 35,000 for the last 3 years. Moreover, the researcher will get a total of Rs 170,000 for the travel, books and other contingencies.

The admission is open for all IITs and the program is managed by IIT Delhi

Applicaion Portal : https://ecampus.iitd.ac.in/ASEAN/login
