Inter Ministerial Committee chaired by Secretary Consumer Affairs holds meeting to review price and availability situation of Onions, Tomatoes and Pulses and augmenting their supplies

The Inter Ministerial Committee held a meeting today under the Chairmanship of Secretary Consumer Affairs to review the price and availability situation of Onions, Tomatoes and Pulses and augment supplies. It was attended by representatives of Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers’ Welfare, Horticulture Commissioner, MD NAFED,Mother Dairy, Kendriya Bhandar, NCCF and senior officials of the Department of Consumer Affairs.
