AAP MLA Alka Lamba expects compensation for Terrorists who died in Balakot : Tweet goes viral

In her tweet originally in Hindi said “Nowadays, number of neutralised terrorists are not counted by number of bodies but through number of active mobile connections. If each terrorist had four mobiles then would he get the compensation of one person or four”.
