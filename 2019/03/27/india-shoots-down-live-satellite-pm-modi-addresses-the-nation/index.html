Surgical Strike in Space, India shoots down live satellite : PM Modi addresses the nation

India becomes the fourth nation to achieve the capability to shoot down live satellites. The Anti-Satellite missile code named as Mission Shakti can shoot down LEO satellites of upto height 300 Kms in just three minutes. Prime Minister Narendra Modi addressed the nation saying “India Shot Down Live Satellite, A Space Power Now”.
