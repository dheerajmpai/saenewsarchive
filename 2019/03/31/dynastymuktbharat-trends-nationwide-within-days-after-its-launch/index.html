#DynastyMuktBharat trends nationwide within days after it’s launch

Congress has faced another backlash as the twitter trend #DynastyMuktBharat is trending nationwide at the #1 position for almost the entire day.

The initiative was formed by pro-democratic activists Shehzad Poonawalla, Chaitanya Sharma, Vagisha Soni on 25th of March 2019. The initiative was taken against the dynastic politics in India
