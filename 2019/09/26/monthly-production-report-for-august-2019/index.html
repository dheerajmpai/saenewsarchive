3.2    Production in JV refineries during August, 2019 was 1655.07 TMT which is 4.22% higher than the target for the month and 39.28% higher when compared with August, 2018. Cumulative production by JVs refineries during April-August, 2019 was 8505.54 TMT which is 8.48% higher than the target for the period and 10.38% higher than the production during corresponding period of last year.

3.3    Production in private refineries during August, 2019 was 7804.71 TMT which is higher by 0.15% than the target both for the month and corresponding month of last year.
