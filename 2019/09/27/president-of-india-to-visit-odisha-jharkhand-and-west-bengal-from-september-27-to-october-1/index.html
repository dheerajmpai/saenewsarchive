President of India to visit Odisha, Jharkhand and west Bengal from September 27 to October 1

The President of India, Shri Ram NathKovind, will visit Odisha, Jharkhand and West Bengal from September 27 to October 1, 2019.

On September 28, 2019, the President will present Colours to Corps of Army Air Defence at Gopalpur, Odisha.

On September 29, 2019, the President will visit Vikas Bharti, Bishunpur, Gumla in Jharkhand.

On September 30, 2019, the President will grace the 33rd convocation of Ranchi University.

On October 1, 2019, the President will visit the National Institute for Locomotor Disabilities in Kolkata, West Bengal.
