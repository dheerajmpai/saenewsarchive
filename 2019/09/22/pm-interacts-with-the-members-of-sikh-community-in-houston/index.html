PM interacts with the members of Sikh community in Houston

Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi today interacted with the members of Sikh community in Houston, Texas. The members of the community accorded a warm welcome to the Prime Minister.

During the interaction, the community members thanked the Prime Minister for the path breaking decisions taken by the Government of India, for the overall development of the country and the Sikh community.

“I had an excellent interaction with the Sikh Community in Houston. I am delighted to see their passion towards India’s development!”, Prime Minister tweeted.
