PM meets the members of Dawoodi Bohra community in Houston, Texas

Prime Minister Narendra Modi today met and interacted with the members of the Dawoodi Bohra community in Houston, Texas.

The community members felicitated the Prime Minister at Houston. During the interaction, the members of Dawoodi Bohra highlighted Prime Minister’s association with Syedna Sahib. They also recalled Narendra Modi’s visit to Indore last year to attend their community program.

Tweeting after the interaction, Prime Minister said, “The Dawoodi Bohra community has distinguished itself across the world. In Houston, I had the opportunity to spend time with them and speak about a wide range of issues.”
