Chintan Shivir concludes with ISPATI IRADA to make India a Vibrant and Competitive Steel Sector

 Chintan Shivir organized by Ministry of Steel on the theme -Towards a Vibrant, Efficient and Globally Competitive Indian Steel sector concluded here today. The purpose of the event was to bring all stakeholders together to deliberate on a roadmap for making Indian Steel Sector more vibrant, efficient and globally competitive. Minister of state for Parliamentary Affairs and Heavy Industries & Public enterprises Shri Arjun Ram Meghwal was the guest of honour on the occasion.

In his address Shri Meghwal said, that steel being a core sector has to play an important role in realising Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s vision of $5 Trillion economy . He further stated that quality of steel determines  the quality of products of all major sectors consuming steel as raw material, especially  heavy engineering goods.To manufacture highest quality of heavy engineering products, highest quality of Steel is required.Thus growth of Indian Steel industry in terms of quality, efficiency and production capabilities is critical for the growth of Heavy Industries,he added.

Speaking on the occasion the Minister of State for Steel Shri Faggan Singh Kulaste said that we are committed to facilitate production capacity augmentation, enhance steel consumption and technology infusion in steel sector. The minister also said that we all must work with ISPATI IRADA to take steel sector to new heights and contribute to the Prime Minister’s vision.
