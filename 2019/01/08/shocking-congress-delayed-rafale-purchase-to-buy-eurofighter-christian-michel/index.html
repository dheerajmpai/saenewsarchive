Just months before the Lok Sabha Election new revelations have emerged on the Rafale Deal.

Agusta Westland middleman Christian Michel has revealed that he was lobbying the UPA govt to purchase Eurofighter over Rafale. The Eurofighter was one of the contestant of the tender along with Rafale.

The Eurofighter lost primarily due to the higher cost and lower performance. The cost of Eurofighter was around 20% more than the Rafale.

After the tender was closed the Eurofighter had offered to reduce the cost by 25% to win the tender. But the IAF rejected the offer outright on the consideration of performance.

This revelation would have a serious consequence on Congress in the upcoming UPA govt.
