MASSIVE #CasteFreeQuota : Modi Govt to give 10 percent Quota to economically backward

Highlights

Prime Minister Narendra Modi made a historic decision to include economically backward classes under reservation.The NDA govt resolved to increase the upper limit of reservation from 50% to 60%.While the first 50% is already reserved for caste based reservation. The remaining 10% would be dedicated for the Economically weaker sections.Every individual with an income of less than 8 Lakhs would get the benefits in education and employment under the new quota policy.There will be no discrimination on the basis of caste/religion . The benefits would apply across all the castes.BJP govt has always been against Caste-based reservation. This move would reinforce their stance on the Vote bank politics.

Prime Minister Modi has sent shock waves from Kashmir to KanyaKumari and Imphal to Ahmedabad in just one stroke. The government has now decided to give 10% Quota for the economically weaker section.(EWS)

This would include all individuals whose annual income is less than 8 Lakhs. People with EWS would get the Quota in the field of education and employment.

This move has come just days after Modi faced an embarrassment in Puduchery when a BJP Karyakartha asked the PM about the schemes for the Middle Class.
