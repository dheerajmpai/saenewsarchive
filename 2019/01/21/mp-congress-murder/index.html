Goondagiri begins : 3 BJP leaders assassinated within 50 days in power.

Highlight

Senior BJP leader Manoj Thakre was found dead in n Barwani district of Madhya Pradesh Sunday morning.This comes within 72 hours after another eminent BJP leader Prahlad Bandhawar, was shot dead in Mandsaur.Two BJP leaders have been shot dead in Madhya Pradesh in past 72 hours. Three since Congress came to power in December.The police have suspected the case as a Murder.Prahlad Bandhawar was the chairman of Mandsaur Municipal Council,Former chief minister Shivraj Singh Chouhan tweeted Sunday that it was “a matter of grave concern that BJP leaders are being killed”
