Former AAP leader Yogendra Yadav bashes ‘Mahagatbhandhan’ : Hollow people with no vision or ideology

Swaraj party chief and former Aam Aadmi Party leader Yogendra Yadav bashed the Kolkata Rally as hollow. He said the ‘Mahagatbhandhan’ is a union of people with no vision or ideology. ‘ All the leaders in the grand alliance, who are portraying themselves as anti-BJP or anti-Modi (Prime Minister Narendra Modi), were equally anti-democracy and corrupt.’ , he added.

He had earlier denied joining the ‘MahaghatBhandhan’ claiming the corrupt background of the parties involved in the alliance.
