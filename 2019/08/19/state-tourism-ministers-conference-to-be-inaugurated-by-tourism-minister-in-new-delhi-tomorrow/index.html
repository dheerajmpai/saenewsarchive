State Tourism Ministers’ Conference to be inaugurated by Tourism Minister in New Delhi tomorrow

The Union Minister of State for Tourism & Culture (Independent Charge),Shri  Prahlad  Singh Patel will inaugurate the  State Tourism Ministers’ Conference being held in New Delhi tomorrow. The daylong Conference has been organized by the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India and will be attended by state Tourism Ministers, Secretaries of Tourism and senior officials from the States and Union Territories (UTs) who will deliberate on various issues related to the development and promotion of Tourism.

The Conference will provide a platform for the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India to apprise the States / UTs about new initiatives taken and those in the pipeline, to get valuable feedback from the States / UTs on the same, to highlight areas where active cooperation of the States / UTs is required and at the same time to learn about the initiatives being taken by the States / UTs for the development of Tourism.
