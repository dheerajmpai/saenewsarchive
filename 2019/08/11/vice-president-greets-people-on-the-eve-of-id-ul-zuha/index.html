Vice President greets people on the eve of Id-ul-Zuha

The Vice President of India, Shri M. Venkaiah Naidu has greeted the people of our country on the auspicious occasion of ‘Id-ul-Zuha’. In a message, he said that the festival is an ode to the virtues of devotion, faith and sacrifice and inspires feelings of brotherhood, compassion and unity.

Following is the text of Vice President’s message:

“I extend my warm greetings to the people of our country on the auspicious occasion of ‘Id-ul-Zuha’.

Id-ul-Zuha, the festival of sacrifice, is celebrated with traditional fervor and gaiety in India and all over the world. The festival is an ode to the virtues of devotion, faith and sacrifice and inspires feelings of brotherhood, compassion and unity.

I hope that the festivities will strengthen the spirit of generosity and bring people closer to one another.

May the noble ideals of Id-ul-Zuha enrich our lives with peace and harmony and bring prosperity to our country.”
