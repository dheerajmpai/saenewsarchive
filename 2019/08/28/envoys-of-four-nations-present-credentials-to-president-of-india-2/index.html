Envoys of Four Nations Present credentials to President of India

High Commissioner of South Africa as well as Ambassadors of China, Somalia and Greece presented credentials to the President of India, Shri Ram Nath Kovind, at a ceremony held at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (August 28, 2019).

 

The envoys who presented their credentials were: –

1. Mr Joel Sibusiso Ndebele, High Commissioner of the Republic of South Africa

2. Mr Sun Weidong, Ambassador of the People’s Republic of China

3. Mrs Faduma Abdullahi Mohamud, Ambassador of the Federal Republic of Somalia

4. Mr Dionyssios Kyvetos, Ambassador of the Greece
