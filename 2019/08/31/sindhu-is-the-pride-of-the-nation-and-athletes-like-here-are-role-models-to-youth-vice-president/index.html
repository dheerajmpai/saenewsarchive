Sindhu is the pride of the nation and athletes like here are role models to youth: Vice President

The Vice President of India, Shri M. Venkaiah Naidu has said PV Sindhu is the pride of the nation and that athletes like here are role models for the youth.

Speaking to media today at his residence in Hyderabad after PV Sindhu, the BWF World Champion, who became the first Indian to win the gold, called on the Vice President along with her parents.

Hailing her hard work, dedication, and commitment of the 24 year Badminton star, he said that she was a great inspiration to all the countrymen.

Complimenting Sindhu for following strict diet, rigorous practice and disciplined practice to achieve her goals, he said the. Fit India call given by the Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi should become a national movement. It was apt, timely and most needed call.

The Vice President said that India has a great demographic dividend with 65% of its population below 35 years of age and observed that it was necessary to have a healthy, active and productive young force to take the country to greater heights.

The Vice President said efforts such as Fit India were extremely relevant at a time when non-communicable diseases were on the rise due to changes in modern lifestyles and dietary habits.

He pointed out that fitness of the body and mind would not only keep individuals alert and smart but also gives them strength to achieve their goals and become socially useful.

He stressed that a healthy nation was a necessary precondition to becoming a wealthy nation.
