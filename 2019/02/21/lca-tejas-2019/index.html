World’s lightest fighter Tejas is now combat ready

DRDO provides ‘Release to service’ certificate to the Light combat aircraft Tejas.

The Light Combat aircraft Tejas, which also happens to be world’s lightest fighter is now ready to be released to the Air Force.

DRDO on Wednesday provided ‘Release to service’ certificate to the Air Force. The Air Force has ordered 40 LCA Tejas Aircrafts to HAL. HAL has already manufactured 10 and will be rolling out the remaining soon.
