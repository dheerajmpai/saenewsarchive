India strikes back : 12 Mirage 2000 cross LoC and destroy terror camps

Indian Army sources have reported ANI that 12 Mirage 2000 jet fighters have crossed LoC and have destroyed the terror launchpads in Pakistan occupied Kashmir. The IAF has struck terror camps in Balakot at around 3.45 am, Muzafarrabad at around 3.48 am and Chakoti at around 3.58 am. Payloads of 1000 Kg Bombs were used and the targets were completely destroyed. The exact number of casualties have not yet been reported by Pakistan.
