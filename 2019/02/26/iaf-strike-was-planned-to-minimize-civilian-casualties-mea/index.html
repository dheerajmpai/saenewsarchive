IAF strike was planned to minimize civilian casualties : MEA

The secretary of Ministry of external affairs briefed in a press conference regarding the recent attack conducted on the terrorist training camps.

He clarified the attack was planned against the JeM training camps and not on Pakistan. The attack was also planned in such a way that maximum terrorists are attacked while the civilian casualty is minimized.
