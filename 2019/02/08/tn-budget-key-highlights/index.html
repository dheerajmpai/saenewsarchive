TN Budget : Key Highlights

TN government concentrated in welfare of farmers. Now Tamilnadu farmers can get water pump in subsidy price.

TN Budget 2019: O Paneerselvam says : Reservoir announced on Orathur tributary on Adyar River as permanent flood mitigation measure along with inter-basin transfer canal. This is to augment water supply and flood mitigation at Orathur, Sriperumbudur.

No new taxes. Integrated Parking Management Project for Chennai city, 2000 electric buses to be procured, 1 lakh houses to be built in Gaja affected areas, Revenue growth of 14% est. and State economic growth of 8.1%. Continuity Budget.

Government will buy 2,000 electric buses and 500 will be purchased very soon, says Finance Minister.

Tamil Nadu’s debt is expected to stand at 3.97 lakh crore , says Deputy Chief Minister O Panneerselvam in his budget speech in the Assembly; expenses for the fiscal 2019-2020 is estimated to be Rs 2 lakh crore.
