Pulwama : Students of MNIT Jaipur pay homage to the Martyrs

Students of Malaviya National Institute of Technology Jaipur paid homage to the martyrs of Pulwama Attack. In response to the Pulwama terrorist attacks by Jaish-e-Mohammed on 14 February on the CPRF convoys, student from MNIT Jaipur participated in the candle light march in huge numbers.
