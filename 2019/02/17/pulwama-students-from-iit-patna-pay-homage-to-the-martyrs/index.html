Pulwama : Students from IIT Patna pay homage to the Martyrs

Students of IIT Patna paid homage to the martyrs of Pulwama Attack. In response to the Pulwama terrorist attacks by Jaish-e-Mohammed on 14 February on the CPRF convoys, student from IIT Patna participated in the candle march in huge numbers Chanting “Bharat Mata ki jai”.
