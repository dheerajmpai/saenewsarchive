Pulwama: IIT Madras students pay respects to fallen CPRF soldiers and condemn terrorist attack

In response to the Pulwama terrorist attacks by Jaish-e-Mohammed on 14 February on the CPRF convoys, IIT Madras students organised a “Shraddhanjali” event showing support to the families of the fallen. Chants of “Vandemataram & Bharat Mata ki Jai” filled the air at Himalaya mess in IIT Madras as the students concluded their candle light march paying homage to the Indian Army and the CRPF.
