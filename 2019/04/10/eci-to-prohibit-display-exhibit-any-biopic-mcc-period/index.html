ECI to prohibit display/exhibit any biopic MCC period

The Election Commission of India today issued an order prohibiting display in electronic media. This includes cinematograph of any biopic or publicity material in the nature of biography/hagiography. These, sub-serving the purposes of any political entity or any individual entity connected to it.

The Election Commission of India (ECI) has stopped the release of biopic ‘PM Narendra Modi’ for the duration of 2019 Lok Sabha elections. The election body said that any material disturbing the level playing field in favour of any political party or individual should not be displayed.
