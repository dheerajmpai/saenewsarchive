Scholarship from NDF

The Government grants scholarship to the children of working (except defence and State Police), retired and deceased defence and para-military force personnel through National Defence Fund.

The Prime Minister’s Scholarship Scheme (PMSS) under the aegis of the National Defence Fund was instituted with effect from 2006-07. Scholarships are available for higher education at technical institutions (medical, dental, veterinary, engineering,MBA and MCA etc.) and are available for a period of four years for each student and 5 years for medical students. The academic requirement for enrolment in the scheme is 60% marks at 10+2 level and 50% marks for renewal each year. The scheme has been extended to the wards of State Police Personnel who are killed during terror/Naxal attacks from the Academic Year 2019-20 onwards.  A budgetary amount of Rs. 1.65 crore has been made for this category for 500 scholarship @ Rs.2500/Rs.3000 for boys/girls respectively. The nodal Ministry for such scholarship will be Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA).
