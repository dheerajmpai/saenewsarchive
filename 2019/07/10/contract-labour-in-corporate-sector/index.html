Contract Labour in Corporate Sector

For the purpose of implementation of the provisions of the various labour laws including the Payment of Wages Act, 1936 and the Minimum Wages Act, 1948, institutional mechanism has been set up in the Central and the State Sphere. The Central Industrial Relations Machinery (CIRM) under the Chief Labour Commissioner (Central){CLC(C)} is entrusted with the responsibility to enforce the provisions of various labour laws in the Central Sphere.
