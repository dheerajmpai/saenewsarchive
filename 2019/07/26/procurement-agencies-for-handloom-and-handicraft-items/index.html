Procurement Agencies for Handloom and Handicraft Items

The purchase preference for local content and local supplier, as envisaged in the Public Procurement Policy notification, is accorded through clause (i) of Rule 153 of General Financial Rules (GFR 2017)of Government of India for handloom items.Year-wise and State-wise details of suppliers and procurement agencies are at Annexure- “A”. A list of handloom items procured by Indian Railways and Ministry of Home affairs (Para-military Forces) is at Annexure – “B”. Also, Ministry of Defence has not procured any handloom items during the above period.
