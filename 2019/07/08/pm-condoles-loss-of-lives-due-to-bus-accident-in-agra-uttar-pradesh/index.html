PM condoles loss of lives due to bus accident in Agra, Uttar Pradesh

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the loss of lives due to the bus accident in Agra, Uttar Pradesh.

“Pained by the bus accident in Agra, Uttar Pradesh. Condolences to the families who have lost their loved ones. I pray that those injured recover fast. The State Government and local administration are providing all possible assistance to the affected”, the Prime Minister said.
