The Government has taken several steps as below:

· Computerization of Defence land records- Raksha Bhoomi Software: The computerization of defence land records under the project ‘Raksha Bhoomi’ was undertaken by the Defence Estates Organization in association with NIC. Defence land records contained in General Land Registers (GLRs) for defence land inside cantonments and Military Land Registers (MLRs) for defence land outside cantonments were entered in the Raksha Bhoomi software which was made operational after verification and authentication of database in July, 2011. The data is updated periodically and shared with user services.

· Archival Unit and Resource Centre (AU&RC): As part of a multi-pronged approach and disaster management strategy, a central facility has been created for preservation and back up and to provide access to all user organizations. An Archival Unit & Resource Center has been operational since 2012 in Raksha Sampada Bhawan New Delhi. Another AU&RC constructed in Kirkee Cantonment for Southern Command is also functioning.

This information was given by Raksha Rajya Mantri Shri Shripad Naik in a written reply to Shri BhagwanthKhuba in Lok Sabha today.
