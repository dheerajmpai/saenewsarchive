Sniper Frontier Competition: 2019

A nine member Indian Army team has left for Belarus to compete in the Sniper Frontier Competition as part of the International Army Games schedule wef 03 Aug to 17 Aug 19. It is a thirteen day intense sniper competition and encompassing flexibility of mind, physical fitness along with firing.

      The competition is likely to have over twenty three participating countries including Russia & China.

      The Lt Gen D Anbu, Vice Chief of Army Staff interacted with the team before they Left for Belarus. The Indian Army team has undergone rigorous training for the competition. We wish them all the best.
