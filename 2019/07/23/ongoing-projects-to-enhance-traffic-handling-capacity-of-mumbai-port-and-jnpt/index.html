Ongoing Projects to enhance traffic handling capacity of Mumbai Port and JNPT

In a written reply to a question in Rajya Sabha today, the Minister of State forShipping (I/C) and Chemical & Fertilizers Shri Mansukh Mandaviya informed that thepresent traffic handling capacity of Mumbai Port and Jawaharlal Nehru Port are 78.4 and 118.3 Million Tonnes Per Annum respectively. He informed thatthe ports have taken up several projects to further enhance their capacity. The details of such projects  are as under:-
