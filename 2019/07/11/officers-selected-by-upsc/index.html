Officers Selected by UPSC

The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) recommends the candidates on the basis of vacancies reported by various Ministries/ Departments/ Cadre Controlling Authorities taking into account the vacancies reserved for Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes and Other Backward Classes, as per reservation roster maintained by them. The details of the officers belonging to General category, Other Backward Classes, Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes recommended by the UPSC during the last three years, are given below:
