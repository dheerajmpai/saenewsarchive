Free Trade and Warehousing Zones in India

There are 8 approved Free Trade and Warehousing Zones (FTWZs) in India.  Out of eight FTWZs, four have been notified.  Out of 4 notified FTWZs, 3 are operational. 

 

There is no investment made by the Central Government in these FTWZs.  Total amount of Rs.3,315.81 crores have been invested by the private Developers/Co-developers in these FTWZs during the last five years.  Further, revenue of Rs.13,051.19 crores have been generated by these FTEZs during the last five years.

 

Setting up of New FTWZs (SEZs) is primarily private investment driven. A statement showing details of these FTWZs in India is given below:
