Sanctioning of Additional Posts for States

The last IAS and IPS Cadre review for Maharashtra State were carried out in 2018 wherein the Total Authorized Strength for IAS was increased from 361 to 415 and for IPS from 302 to 317.  Similarly, the last IAS and IPS Cadre review for Rajasthan State were carried out in 2016 and 2015 respectively, wherein the Total Authorized for IAS was increased from 296 to 313 and for IPS from 205 to 215.
