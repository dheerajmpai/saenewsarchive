Increase in allocation of funds for tribal welfare

The extant Guidelines of NITI Aayog prescribes for earmarking of dedicated funds for tribal welfare by Central Ministries / Departments out of total scheme outlay (Centrally Sponsored Schemes and Central Sector Schemes) of that Ministry / Department at different rates asgiven below.

In the year 2014-15, total obligated Ministries / Departments were 28 which have increased to 40 in the year 2018-19.  Central fund for tribal welfare increases with the increase in obligated central Ministries and their scheme outlay. There has been consecutive increase in allocation of central funds during 2014-15 to 2018-19 as tabulated below:
