Road Ministry Stresses fitment of Under-Run Protection in Heavy Goods Vehicles across The Country

The Ministry of Road Transport and Highways has issued advisory to state governments reiterating the need for fitment of Under-Run Protection in heavy goods carrying vehicles and trailers throughout the country. In a communication to all States and Union Territories, the Ministry has requested that mandatory regulation for Rear Under-run protecting Device for N2, N3, T3 and T4 category of vehicles should be strictly complied with.

The Ministry had notified vide G.5.R.677(E) dated 03.09.2015 mandating Rear Under-run protecting Device for N2, N3, T3 and T4 category of vehicles which include heavy goods carrier vehicles and trailers. It was, however, noticed that scores of heavy vehicles and trailers were still running without Under-Run Protecting Device which is meant to guard vehicles in case of collision/accidents and can greatly reduce fatalities and serious injuries.
