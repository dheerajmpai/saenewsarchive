Training to Men, Women and Persons with Disability

Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship through National Skill  Development and Corporation promotes establishment of model and aspirational skill centres known as Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendra (PMKK) in every district for imparting skill training through Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) 2016-20. State Governments are expected to facilitate the establishment of PMKKs, by provision of land etc. Targets under Centrally Sponsored State Managed – PMKVY 2016-20 may be allocated to PMKKs by State Governments.

Under the skill ecosystem, the concerned Sector Skill Council (SSC) develop sector specific QPs/NOS for various job roles. Some courses/job roles developed by concerned SSCs which are in high demand are kept under PMKVY for imparting skill development training.
