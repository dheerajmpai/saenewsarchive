PM greets Chartered Accountants on Chartered Accountants’ Day

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has greeted Chartered Accountants, on Chartered Accountants’ Day.

“The hardworking fraternity of Chartered Accountants has been furthering the culture of honesty and better corporate governance in our society. CAs also play a key role in advancing economic prosperity. Today, on CA Day, my best wishes to all CAs for their future endeavours”, the Prime Minister said.
