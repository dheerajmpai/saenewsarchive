Steel Plants

The State-wise number and details of the Steel plants established by the Government and other enterprises in the country at present are given at Annexure.

To promote a Steel SPV in the state of  Jharkhand, an MoU between Ministry of Steel, Government of Jharkhand and NMDC was signed on 28th June, 2015.  Owing to non-allocation of captive iron ore mining lease by the State Government, the steel plant could not be set up.

This information was given by Minister of Steel, Shri Dharmendra Pradhan in a written reply to the Lok Sabha today.
