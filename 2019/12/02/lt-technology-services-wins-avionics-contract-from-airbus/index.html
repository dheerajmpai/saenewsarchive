L&T Technology Services wins avionics contract from Airbus

L&T Technology Services will now be managing avionics software development, validation and verification, and data analytics for Airbus India.

The direct win has been attributed to L&TTS’ years of expertise in the aviation space and the company’s timely investment in cutting-edge technologies. Although no further information on time frame or value of the contract has been divulged.

The company sees a distinct advantage in the aerospace landscape with ITAR (International Traffic in Arms) compliance and CEMILAC (The Centre for Military Airworthiness and Certification) certified state-of-the-art facilities.

L&T Technology Services Ltd is a listed subsidiary of Larsen & Turbo focused on engineering and R&D. It offers consultancy, design, development and testing services across the product and process development life cycle.
