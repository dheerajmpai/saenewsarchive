Social Activist Payal Rohatgi arrested for commenting about Nehru

The Rajasthan Police has arrested Payal Rohatgi for posting a video commenting on Jawaharlal Nehru and his family. The actress turned social activist had posted a video on Nehru-Gandhi Family that described the family tree including the relationships and alleged affairs of Motilal Nehru, Jawaharlal Nehru, Indira Gandhi, Feroz Gandhi, Sanjay Gandhi and Rahul Gandhi. In the later part of the video she also mentioned about the nepotism in the Nehru Gandhi Family and also included remarks about Robert Vadra’s increase in wealth.

The video was first posted on twitter on 21st September and has been removed right now.

The Rajasthan Police have detained her at Bundi. She has been sent to eight day judicial custody by a local court in Bundi. She was detained in Bundi where she lived along with other cellmates, most of them accused for murder and robbery.
