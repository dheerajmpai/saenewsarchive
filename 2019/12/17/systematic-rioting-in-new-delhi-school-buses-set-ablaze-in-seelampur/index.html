Systematic rioting in New Delhi : School buses set ablaze in Seelampur

Systematic riots by the Anti-CAA protesters have set the national capital on fire. Thousands of protestors have pelted stones towards the security personnel in Seelampur Delhi. The Delhi Metro has been suspended in the north east part of the city.

The rioters have set a Police van and a school bus on fire. No casualties have been reported yet. The rioters have been found chasing the police and pelting stones towards them. Megha Prasad, the TimesNow correspondent of Prime Minister office published the video of rioting in New Delhi on twitter.

The Video also covered rioters setting fire to the school bus which could have potentially boarded school children.
