Thousands of BHU students took to street in support of Citizenship Amendment Act

More than thousand students of Indian Institute of Technology took to street to support the Citizenship amendment act that provides citizenship for the persecuted minorities from neighbouring Islamic countries. The students held the banner that said “banaras Hindu University” stands with CAB and NRC. The students marched through the streets of the University and finally entered the main circle of the campus. The march was conducted in a peaceful way and several volunteers had interactions with the common public to raise awareness about the Citizenship Amendment Act.

This happens just two days after the crowd from Jamia Millia Islamia rioted in Delhi and set four buses on fire.
