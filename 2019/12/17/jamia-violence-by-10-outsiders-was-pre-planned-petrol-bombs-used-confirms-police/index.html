Jamia violence by 10 outsiders was pre-planned, petrol bombs used : confirms Police

The Police probe on the Jamia Millia Islamia violence confirmed that the attacks were pre-planned. The probe shows that the four buses were set to fire at different locations within a duration of thirty minutes. The police have also confirmed the use of petrol bombs to set the buses ablaze.

In an interview with the TImes Now the Delhi police also confirmed that there were 10 people who were involved in the violence. All the ten accused turn out to be outsiders and not affiliated to the university in any way. They have also confirmed that all the 10 accused have criminal backgrounds.

One of the interviewee also mentioned that the India’s students are being “Used” by these goons to put forward their political agenda. Some of the twitteratis also observed that the same woman appeared in every protests which clearly shows the protests and violence were pre-planned.
