Pinarayi goes the Trump way : says Kerala cannot accept foreign refugees under CAB.

Kerala Chief Minister Pinarayi Vijayan on Thursday, while addressing a press conference, announced that his state will not implement The Citizen (Amendment) Bill, 2019 passed by the Parliament.

Both Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha passed The Citizenship (Amendment) Bill that provides Indian citizenship to the six minority communities – Hindu, Buddhist, Jain, Parsi, Christian and Sikhs who have sought refuge in India from religious persecution in Bangladesh, Afghanistan and Pakistan.

Kerala Chief Minister Pinarayi has given the statement in similar lines of US President Donald Trump who has refused to retain the refugees from Mexico and South America.

More than 3 Million refugees from Bangladesh, Pakistan and Afghanistan would recieve the benefit of Citizenship Amendment Bill that was passed on December 12th.
