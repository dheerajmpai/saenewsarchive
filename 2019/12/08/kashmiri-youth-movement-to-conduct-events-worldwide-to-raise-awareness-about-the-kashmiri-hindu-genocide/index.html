Kashmiri Youth movement to conduct events worldwide to raise awareness about the Kashmiri Hindu Genocide

Kashmiri Youth Movement, a non-political student group of young Kashmiris born post Kashmiri Pandit exodus, is conducting events across the world to raise awareness about the Kashmiri Hindu genocide. The first event would be held in Delhi at Connaught Place on 8th of December. The upcoming event are to be held in New York, Los Angeles and several other places in USA, Canada, Australia and New Zealand.

The Kashmiri Hindu exodus is one of the bloodiest exodus the nation ever faced. The Kashmiri Hindu community faced seven waves of exodus since the first one that took place in 1300 to the seventh one in 1990s. More than a million were displaced from their homeland and are living as refugees in multiple parts of the world. Yet it is often neglected by the media and also by the Indian government. Till date, textbooks nor the mainstream media mention the atrocities faced by the Kashmiri Pandits.
