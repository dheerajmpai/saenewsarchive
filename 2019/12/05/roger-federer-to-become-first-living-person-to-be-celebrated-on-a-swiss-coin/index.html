Roger Federer to become first living person to be celebrated on a Swiss coin

The Federal Mint, Swissmint is honouring 20-time Grand Slam champion Roger Federer on 20 Swiss francs silver commemorative coins, official dispatch of which will start January 23. It plans to add a Federer Swiss francs 50 gold coin in May 2020.

Of the 35,000 20-franc silver coins offered in the pre-sale window, 15,000 have already been taken, the remaining are expected to sell quickly once the website returns to full functionality.

This is the first time in its history that the Swissmint has created a commemorative coin to honour a living person.
