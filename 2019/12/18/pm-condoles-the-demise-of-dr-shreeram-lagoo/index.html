PM condoles the demise of Dr. Shreeram Lagoo

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the demise of Dr. Shreeram Lagoo.

“Dr. Shreeram Lagoo personified versatility and brilliance. Through the years, he enthralled audiences with outstanding performances. His work will be remembered for years to come. Anguished by his demise. Condolences to his admirers. Om Shanti”, the Prime Minister said.
